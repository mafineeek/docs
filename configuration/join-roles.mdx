# Join Roles

Automatically assign roles to members when they join your server.

## Adding a Join Role

To start, click the **Add Role** button.

![Add Role](https://cdn.murffy.xyz/docs/brave_RXimK4yXqw.png)

### Configuration

In the modal that appears, you can configure the following:

- **Role**: Select the role you want to assign.
- **Bots Only**:
  - `True`: The role will ONLY be assigned to bots that join the server.
  - `False`: The role will be assigned to normal users.

![Role Configuration](https://cdn.murffy.xyz/docs/brave_5ngj1tG4us.png)

## Managing Roles

Once added, you can view all active join roles in the list. You can remove a role at any time by clicking the trash icon next to it.

![Manage Roles](https://cdn.murffy.xyz/docs/brave_SP5UcWw015.png)
