# Custom Bot
With our Premium plan, you can customize the bot's appearance to match your community's brand, making it feel like a seamless part of your server.

## Accessing Custom Instance
To access the custom bot settings, navigate to your server dashboard and look for the **Custom Instance** tab in the Premium section.

![Custom Instance Tab](https://cdn.murffy.xyz/docs/custom-bot-1.png)

## Customization Options
Once in the Custom Instance section, you can fully personalize your bot's profile.

![Custom Bot Settings](https://cdn.murffy.xyz/docs/custom-bot-2.png)

You have the ability to change the following:

- **Bot Name**: Give your bot a unique name that fits your server.
- **Bot Bio**: Write a custom description or bio for your bot's profile.
- **Bot Avatar**: Upload a custom icon to replace the default avatar.
- **Bot Banner**: Set a custom banner image for the bot's profile.
